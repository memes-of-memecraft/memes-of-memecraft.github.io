Memes of Memecraft Memefesto

We the Memers of Memecraft, together in humanitys dankest hour, establish the following manifesto for the Mother of Memesthe Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO) overseeing the Memes of Memecraft (MoM).

A meme is worth a thousand words. These magical memes wake revolutions, win elections, move mountains, and make millionaires. Whether we like it or not, memes define our lives as they shape our culture. We live in the wild west of multiplicitous memes.

Sun Tzu said: in the midst of chaos, there is also opportunity. There is no consensus on the dankest, spiciest, cutest, or shittiest memes. Memes of Memecraft will change this. A purely peer-to-peer proof of meme awaits.

Memes of Memecraft is a pioneer in digital meme ownership. Thousands of unique memes will be created by MoM DAO, Mother of Memes, and owned by fellow MoM memers. Each meme is irrevocably issued, forever to live in the Ethereum cyberspace.

Memes of Memecraft has no leader. The great Mother of Memes knows no limits. Each memer equal in the eyes of MoM.

With great power comes great responsibility.

We meme harm, and we meme the best.
